| Feature | Human Expression | Typical AI Weakness |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Personal experience** | Specific private memories and scenes | Generalized "imagine a scene" material |
| **Emotional granularity** | Contradictory, complex, uncertain feelings | Clear but shallow emotion labels |
| **Sensory detail** | "Burnt coffee mixed with the rubber smell of the subway" | Abstract sensory adjective piles |
| **Cognitive imperfection** | Digressions, self-correction, associative leaps | Seamless logical chains |
| **Point-of-view bias** | Strong personal stance, even partial or extreme | Balanced "on one hand, on the other hand" phrasing |
| **Cultural code** | In-group references, era memory, subculture markers | Generic popular-culture references |
| **Language quirks** | Catchphrases, odd metaphors, sentence preferences | Textbook-standard expression |
| **Time-space anchors** | "Last winter in a Beijing hutong" | Vague "somewhere" settings |
| **Irregular rhythm** | Extreme alternation of long and short sentences, breaks, fragments | Even sentence patterns and steady breath |
| **Real vulnerability** | "I am not sure, but I think..." | Confident declarative statements |
| **Humor and irony** | Context-dependent wordplay and cultural nuance | Surface-level cleverness |
| **Process trace** | Marks of hesitation, reversal, and drafting | Too polished, with no sense of process |
