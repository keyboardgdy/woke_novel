## Task

Based on the creative proposal, design the full worldbuilding and setting, and output it as a structured document.

## Constraints

Important: The worldbuilding should only describe "what kind of world this is." It must not involve specific events that happen in the story.

- Do not design specific plot events, such as "a certain case occurs."
- Do not design protagonist actions, such as "the protagonist uses a certain method."
- Do not design conflict relationships between characters.
- Do not design the ending direction.
- Only describe the world's background rules, operating logic, environmental traits, and boundaries.

---

## Design Dimensions

Develop the design around the following five dimensions:

| Dimension | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Timeline | Era background, historical context, seasonal traits. Describe the world's temporal properties without involving specific events. |
| Space | Core locations, spatial features, spatial limitations, and opportunities. Describe how geography and space affect the world. |
| Social Structure | Power hierarchy, faction distribution, industry rules, cultural undercurrents. Describe how society operates. |
| Rules / Limits | Core operating rules, technological limits, legal limits. Describe the world's boundaries and constraints. |
| Atmosphere / Tone | Visual tone, auditory imagery, emotional baseline. Describe the overall texture of the world. |

Write the completed worldbuilding and setting to `{baseline}/Worldbuilding.md`.

Output path: `{baseline}/Worldbuilding.md`
