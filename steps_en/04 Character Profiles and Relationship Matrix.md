## Task

Based on the creative proposal and worldbuilding, generate core character profiles and a character relationship matrix, then output them as structured JSON files.

---

## Constraints

Important: Character profiles should only describe "what kind of people the characters are." They must not involve specific story events.

- Do not design specific actions or decisions by characters.
- Do not design specific conflict events between characters.
- Do not design character growth arcs inside the story.
- Do not design character behavior in particular scenes.
- Only describe background, personality, abilities, motivation type, and original relationships with other characters.

1. Write the character profiles to `{chars}/Character_Profiles.json`.
2. Write the relationship matrix to `{chars}/Relationship_Matrix.json`.

Output path: `{chars}/Character_Profiles.json`

Output path: `{chars}/Relationship_Matrix.json`
